How to Upload to Pebblely.com (Free Account)

Step-by-Step Guide

  1. Go to www.pebblely.com.
  2. Click on “Sign Up” or “Try for free”.
  3. Create a free account using your email or Google login.
  4. Once logged in, click on the “Upload” or “Create” button on the dashboard.
  5. Choose an image (e.g., product photo or object with a transparent/solid background) from your computer.
  6. Wait for the image to upload. Pebblely will then show you AI-generated background options.
  7. Select your favorite result or click “Generate More” for more variations.
  8. Click the Download button to save the image (some limits may apply on free plans).
💡 Tips: Use PNG images with transparent backgrounds for better results. Free accounts have limited generations per month.
